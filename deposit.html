Deposits

A deposit is required to secure all hair extension installation appointments including consultation-approved services. After your consultation, an invoice will be provided that outlines the exact cost of the hair product and installation.

The deposit consists of the hair product cost as agreed upon in the consultation, and is due before ordering the product from our supplier.

Once the deposit is paid:

Appointments are considered confirmed and production begins. Because materials are custom-ordered and matched to your specifications, hair orders and deposits are non-refundable.

You acknowledge and agree that by submitting a deposit you accept this non-refund policy. This ensures I can secure the exact hair you need and dedicate my time exclusively to your transformation.
